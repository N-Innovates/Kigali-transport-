Explore the Research

The Challenge We Face

Kigali's rapid population growth has strained our public transportation system, creating significant challenges for daily commuters.

📈

Growing Population

Kigali's population has grown exponentially, but public transport infrastructure hasn't kept pace with demand.

⏰

Extended Wait Times

Commuters face 45-60 minute delays daily, significantly impacting productivity and quality of life.

😰

Stress & Overcrowding

Overcrowded buses and unpredictable schedules create stress and discomfort for passengers.

👥

Most Affected Groups

Students, low-income workers, and small business owners bear the greatest burden of these transportation challenges.

PESTLE Analysis

Examining the external factors that influence Kigali's public transportation challenges.

🏛️

Political

Government policies, urban planning decisions, and public investment priorities directly shape transport infrastructure development.

💰

Economic

Limited public funds, fare affordability concerns, and economic growth creating increased transport demand.

👥

Social

Rapid urbanization, changing commuter expectations, and social inequality affecting transport access.

💻

Technological

Opportunities for GPS tracking, mobile apps, and smart traffic management systems to improve efficiency.

⚖️

Legal

Transport regulations, safety standards, and licensing requirements affecting service quality and innovation.

🌱

Environmental

Air quality concerns, climate change goals, and the need for sustainable transport solutions.
